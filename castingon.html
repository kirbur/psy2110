The basic construction of a garter stitch scarf starts with the cast on. At this stage you need to decide how wide you want your scarf to be, if you are unsure, then my suggestion would be about 8 inches. You should look at your yarn label and determine what the stitch gauge is. You will use this number to determine how many stitches you need to cast on.
