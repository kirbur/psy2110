Intro

Knitting is a way of making fabric using 2 sticks and a piece of yarn. The repetitive nature of knitting makes a very relaxing and stress relieving passtime. It can be very rewarding to create wearable items with your own hands.

Objectives

After this lesson, you will be able to:

  • Apply an understanding of knitting tools and yarn to pick your own,
  • Demonstate a basic cast on,
  • Demonstate the knit stitch,
  • Demonstate the purl stitch,
  • Demonstate a simple bind off,
  • Combine the previous skills to make a scarf,
  • and Appreciate knitting as a hobby.

All of the knitting skills will be taught using the continental style of knitting.
