In this lesson you will learn how to cast on, the knit stitch, and how to bind off. Using only the knit stitch means that the we will be knitting in a pattern called "garter stitch". Garter stitch is one of the most commonly used stitch patterns because of its simplicity. You have seen garter stitch on every page of this lesson so far, because it is used as the background.
